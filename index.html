Privacy Policy

CareWise should collect the minimum health data needed, encrypt protected fields, avoid health data in logs, and explain data use before signup.

Terms of Service

Users must understand this is a care-planning tool, not emergency care, diagnosis, prescription, or a replacement for licensed professionals.

Medical Disclaimer

AI output is educational and must route high-risk cases to licensed clinicians or emergency services. It should not claim to cure disease.

Data Deletion Request

Users need a clear way to request deletion of account, report, consent, and profile records before app store review.
