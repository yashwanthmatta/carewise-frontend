Data Deletion

CareWise Data Deletion Request

Last updated: June 19, 2026

How To Request Deletion

Signed-in users can use the CareWise data portability section to request deletion or delete account data through the backend self-service endpoint.

What Deletion Covers

  • Account record and authentication tokens.
  • Patient profile fields, medications, intake records, and care plans.
  • Uploaded report records and private report storage objects where available.
  • Consent records, notification preferences, subscriptions, and audit events linked to the account.

Operational Notes

Some records may be retained when required for legal, security, fraud-prevention, billing, backup, or compliance purposes. Production retention rules should be reviewed by legal and privacy professionals before public launch.

Support

If the in-app deletion workflow is unavailable, users should contact CareWise support with the account email and deletion request details.
