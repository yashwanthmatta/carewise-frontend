CareWise AI Medical Disclaimer

Last updated: June 19, 2026

Educational Use Only

CareWise AI provides educational summaries and care navigation support. It is not a medical device, clinical diagnosis tool, prescription system, or emergency service.

Professional Review Required

Users should review CareWise output with licensed healthcare professionals, especially for symptoms, abnormal reports, medications, pregnancy, chronic conditions, children, older adults, or high-risk cases.

AI Can Be Wrong

AI-generated information can be incomplete, outdated, or incorrect. CareWise should route high-risk situations to clinician review and should never claim 100% accuracy.

Emergency Warning

If a user has severe symptoms, chest pain, trouble breathing, stroke signs, severe allergic reaction, suicidal thoughts, uncontrolled bleeding, or other urgent concerns, they should seek emergency care immediately.
