Privacy Policy

CareWise AI Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

CareWise AI is a care navigation MVP. This policy explains what data may be collected, why it is used, and how users can request access or deletion.

Data We Collect

  • Account details such as email, role, verification state, and login session records.
  • Patient profile information entered by the user, such as name, region, conditions, allergies, medications, goals, diet style, and activity level.
  • Uploaded reports and extracted report text when the user chooses to upload or paste health information.
  • Consent records, audit events, subscription state, notification preferences, and deletion requests.

How Data Is Used

  • To create educational care summaries, habit plans, diet suggestions, and clinician-review workflows.
  • To help users organize reports, medication notes, insurance guidance, and doctor questions.
  • To secure accounts, verify email ownership, process password resets, and maintain audit history.

Storage And Security

CareWise is designed to store sensitive report files in private cloud storage, encrypt selected patient fields, avoid health data in logs, and keep audit trails for protected actions. No internet system can guarantee perfect security.

AI And Health Information

AI output may be incomplete or wrong. CareWise should be reviewed by qualified professionals before public healthcare use and should not be used as emergency care, diagnosis, cure, prescription, or a replacement for licensed medical judgment.

User Choices

  • Users can request data export and deletion from the CareWise app.
  • Users can avoid uploading reports and may paste only the text they choose to share.
  • Users should contact CareWise support for privacy requests that cannot be completed in the app.
